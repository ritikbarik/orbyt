How It Works

Get Started with Orbyt in 5 Easy Steps

Step 1: Sign Up

Verify your college email address (.edu) to ensure you're part of the Orbyt campus community and unlock access to all features.

Step 2: Create Your Profile

Showcase your personality! Add photos, share your major, list your interests, and write a bio to attract the right connections.

Step 3: Match

Browse profiles and connect with students who share your vibe. Orbyt's matching algorithm helps you find your tribe on campus.

Step 4: Chat

Break the ice and start conversations with your new connections. Discuss classes, plan study sessions, or simply hang out!

Step 5: Engage

Explore campus events, join study groups, share your experiences in Campus Stories, and connect with the Orbyt community.
