About Orbyt

Our Mission

Orbyt is on a mission to revolutionize campus connections by helping college students build meaningful friendships, find dedicated study buddies, and discover exciting campus eventsâ€”all within a single, intuitive app.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every college student feels deeply connected, fully supported, and actively engaged within their campus community. We believe that a strong sense of belonging is the foundation for academic success and personal well-being.

Problems We Solve

Orbyt is designed to address key challenges faced by college students today:

  • Combating Loneliness: Helping students, especially freshmen, build connections and find their tribe.
  • Streamlining Study Buddy Search: Providing an efficient and effective way to find study partners in specific majors and courses.
  • Creating a Campus Hub: Offering a dedicated social platform tailored to the unique needs of college students.
  • Boosting Campus Engagement: Making it easier for students to discover and participate in events, groups, and activities.
  • Facilitating Resource Sharing: Providing a marketplace for students to buy, sell, and trade resources affordably.
  • Providing Mentorship Opportunities: Connecting students with mentors for valuable guidance and support.

Who is Orbyt For?

Orbyt is designed for all college students looking to enhance their campus experience, especially:

  • Freshmen & Transfer Students: Seeking to build a new social network.
  • Students Seeking Academic Support: Looking for study buddies and resources.
  • Those Seeking Mentorship: Wanting guidance and advice from experienced peers.
  • Buyers & Sellers: Interested in affordable textbooks and other resources.
  • Event Enthusiasts: Eager to discover and participate in campus activities.
